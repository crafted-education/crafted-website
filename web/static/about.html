Our Culture

We believe…

…in a sense of purpose, with great customers and great colleagues.
…that we are all in this together. We trust each other. We challenge each other. These are not opposites.
…in compasses (not maps), autonomy, accountability, and the importance of continuous improvement
…that success can and should be measured.
…in open source and giving back to the technology community.

Above all - we believe success comes through a willingness to teach and learn. We are on a mission to help educate the world - ourselves included.

We believe success comes through educating people. We believe this is a noble cause.
