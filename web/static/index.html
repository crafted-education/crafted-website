What is crafted education?

crafted education is a project based consulting company with decades of experience creating impactful solutions in the education technology market. We help organizations define and execute their education technology strategies by providing full stack planning, design, development, testing and delivery services.

We believe success comes through educating people. We believe this is a noble cause.

Why us?

crafted isn’t your normal tech consulting company.

We provide a full stack team that can work through planning, design, architecture, development, testing and delivery services. That means you don’t have to provide dedicated support roles such as product management, project management or user experience. That doesn’t mean we don’t work with those teams in your organization! Our planning and design methodology is built on a collaborative process that ensures we can accurately represent your vision, priorities, voice, style and brand.

Have you ever had someone build something that you couldn’t support? We have. And we won’t do that to you. We will build in the language you choose, and provide you with the education, documentation, training and support you need to take it in house when you’re ready.
