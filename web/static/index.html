Crafted Education is a project based consulting company with decades of experience creating impactful solutions in the education technology market. We provide a full suite of product planning, design, development, testing, and delivery services to help your organization execute on your technology projects. Our team is on a mission to make quality education more accessible, relevant and engaging for every learner.

New Product Innovation

Validating new product ideas can be time consuming and expensive. We know how to take a new product concept through ideation, prototype and initial market delivery so you can quickly determine if the idea is worth investing in for the long term.

Product & System Integration

Ed tech is all about integration. Whether it’s the tools you’ve built in house, capabilities you’ve acquired or third parties you need to integrate - we know how to bring them together in a way that makes sense to you and your end users. And we’ve spent a lot of time with IMS and SIF - we know those standards and we know when and how to make them work.

Mobile App & Mobile Web

Do you have a site or product that needs to go mobile? We can help you get there with native app, mobile web and responsive design development.

Crafted Education isn’t your normal tech consulting company.

We get education. In fact, our leadership team has more than 30 years of experience working in technology for K-12, Higher Ed and Professional Education. More experience means less ramp time, faster delivery and better quality solutions for your organization.

We aren’t just about development. We’ll help you all the way through product definition, user experience, architecture, development, testing and delivery. And we won’t build you something you can’t support. We’ll listen to your technology needs and provide you with the education, documentation, and training you need to take it in house when you’re ready.

We know how to run fast. Innovative ideas can’t wait 12 months to get prioritized, staffed and built. We can take your new product from concept to working software in weeks - helping you stay ahead of the curve without blowing up your existing roadmap.
